SIGNCONNECT- Bridging Communication Gaps with Sign Language
SignConnect is an innovative web application designed to bridge communication gaps by serving as a real-time translator for Indian Sign Language (ISL). Tailored to empower individuals with hearing and speech disabilities, SignConnect seamlessly converts ISL gestures into text and speech, and vice versa, fostering more inclusive communication. By focusing solely on ISL and eliminating the need for user logins, the platform ensures a streamlined and user-friendly experience. With its emphasis on accessibility and ease of use, SignConnect aspires to break barriers and promote seamless interaction between differently-abled individuals and the wider community.
